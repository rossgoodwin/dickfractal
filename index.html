Bleeding Edge Research

Our field was founded on the claim that a central property of humans, intelligence -- the sapience of Homo sapiens -- can be so precisely described that a machine can be made to simulate it. This raises philosophical issues about the nature of the mind and the ethics of creating artificial beings endowed with human-like intelligence, issues which have been addressed by myth, fiction and philosophy since antiquity. Artificial intelligence has been the subject of tremendous optimism, but has also suffered stunning setbacks. Today it has become an essential part of the technology industry, providing the heavy lifting for many of the most challenging problems in computer science.

Maximum Automation

Make as much as possible automatic: you write a little code, and it automatically selects algorithms, organizes data, creates interfaces, optimizes presentation and sets up production deployment.

Entity Extraction

Named entity extraction is capable of identifying people, companies, organizations, cities, geographic features and other typed entities from your HTML, text or web-based content.

Sentiment Analysis

Sentiment analysis is capable of computing document-level sentiment, sentiment for a user-specified target, entity-level sentiment, quotation-level sentiment, directional-sentiment and keyword-level sentiment.

Image Tagging

Quickly categorize and organize image libraries at a massive scale. By understanding complex visual scenes in their broader context, you can automatically extract knowledge from images and act upon what you learn.

Facial Recognition

Returns the position, age, gender, and, in the case of celebrities, the identities of the people in the photo.

Taxonomy

Deeper levels allow you to classify content into more accurate and lucrative subsegments. For instance, an application focused on identifying content discussing personal lending practices can narrow its classification into sub topics that target decisions with finer resolution.
